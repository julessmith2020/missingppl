Who in Utah?

Cases that have been reported show a little about the victims and who has been targeted.


CTDC Age of Victims

  • 1/3 of children trafficked are between ages 15-17
  • More than 50% of girls trafficked are between 15-17
  • Approximately 40% of boys trafficked are under 12
  • While adults are trafficked more into agriculture and manufacturing, children are more often trafficked into begging, peddling and illicit activities. Traffickers will send children out to beg or sell things on the street, and even carry out theft and other crimes.
  • Over 40% of children were recruited by a family member or relative
  • Many trafficked children will never received a high school education
